Post App Build Checklist

This checklist is implemented for you to carry out the post-app-build tasks. Please complete all necessary steps.

  • Verify the build (test whether the .dmg, .exe and .deb works correctly)
  • Upload build to the client's website.
  • Upload the build to SharePoint.
  • Upload the project backup to SharePoint.
  • Update IPS Data.
  • Add notes to IPS Data if needed.
